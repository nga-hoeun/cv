My Childhood

Field Work

My family owned large amount of farmland when I was a child. In my free time from schooling, I helped my family plant rice, harvest rice, and many others fields of plantation.

Herding Cattles

My family also owned cows. We used them to plow the fields and many other jobs. My duty was to herding them and took care of them. It got reallly exciting when we herd the cow as a group and also spending time with our friends.

Study
